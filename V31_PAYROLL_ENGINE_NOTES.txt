HR Glass Pro V31 - Hourly Payroll Engine

New payroll rule:
- Monthly salary is converted to hourly rate.
- Default formula:
  hourlyRate = salary / 30 / dailyHours
- Example:
  salary = 3000
  dailyHours = 8
  day value = 3000 / 30 = 100
  hourlyRate = 100 / 8 = 12.5
- Daily / Weekly / Monthly / Hourly settlement is calculated from actual attendance hours.
- If the employee has no attendance hours in the selected period, base salary for that period = 0.
- Advances, deductions, bonuses, and previous payments are still applied from transactions.
- Salary payment/settlement subtracts from the remaining net balance.
- Print receipt layout was redesigned for professional A4 output.

Dark mode:
- Improved text contrast in cards, forms, details, and payroll areas.
