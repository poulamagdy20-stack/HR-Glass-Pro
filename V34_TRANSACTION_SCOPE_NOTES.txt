HR Glass Pro V34 - Payroll Transaction Scope

New:
- Manual payroll transaction now has a Transaction Scope:
  Auto by date / Daily / Weekly / Monthly

How it works:
- Daily transaction appears in the selected day and rolls up in weekly/monthly totals.
- Weekly transaction appears in weekly totals and rolls up in monthly totals.
- Monthly transaction appears only in monthly/hourly month view.
- Auto by date keeps old behavior: included whenever its date is inside the selected period.

This applies to:
- Advances
- Deductions
- Bonuses
- Absence deductions
- Adjustments
