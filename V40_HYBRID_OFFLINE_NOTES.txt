HR Glass Pro V40 - Hybrid Offline Storage

Added:
- IndexedDB local queue for offline writes.
- If device is offline or Firebase is unavailable, new attendance/requests/transactions/settings/shifts/employees are queued locally.
- When internet returns, queued data syncs to Firebase automatically.
- Top bar sync indicator:
  Online / Offline / Sync pending.
- Button consistency improved for mobile and desktop.

Important:
- Existing data still lives in Firebase Firestore as the primary database.
- Offline queued data is stored inside the browser IndexedDB until synced.
- Do not clear browser storage before sync.
